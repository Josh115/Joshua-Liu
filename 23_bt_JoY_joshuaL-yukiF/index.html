Color

.bg-color can be used to set the Background of the element.
For Example, if you wanted the Background of a div to blue:
<div class="bg-primary"> . . . </div> Bootstrap doesn't have every color readily available, but they do have:

  • primary > blue
  • secondary > gray-600
  • success > green
  • info > cyan
  • warning > yellow
  • danger > red
  • light > gray-100
  • dark > gray-900
Additionally, .text-color can be used to change the color of the text in the element, For Example:
<div class="bg-primary text-white"> . . . </div>    --- > Blue Background, white text
<p class="text-secondary"> . . . </p>    --- > Grey text
